Cilantro

Cairo, Egypt

Cilantro is a modern Egyptian coffeehouse chain that blends comfort with style. With its welcoming ambiance, diverse beverage menu, and fresh bakery items, Cilantro is a popular spot for coffee lovers, students, professionals, and casual meetings. Whether you're looking for a cozy place to relax, catch up on work, or meet with friends, Cilantro offers an inviting atmosphere and reliable Wi-Fi across its many branches.

Features

  • - Wheelchair-accessible entrances and seating
  • - Accessible restrooms in selected branches
  • - Staff trained to assist customers with disabilities
  • - Clean, well-maintained facilities
  • - Freshly brewed coffee and handcrafted beverages
