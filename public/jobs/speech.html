Sign Language Instructor

A Sign Language Instructor teaches sign language to individuals, groups, or organizations. This can be done in-person or online using visual aids and live demonstrations. The role focuses on educating others on effective non-verbal communication and Deaf culture awareness.

ASL Video Content Creator

An ASL (American Sign Language) Video Content Creator is someone who creates and shares video content using American Sign Language to communicate with the deaf and hard-of-hearing community. These creators produce educational, informative, or entertainment videos that feature ASL to help bridge communication gaps

Lab Assistant (Non-verbal Communication)

A Lab Assistant supports scientific or medical labs by preparing materials, conducting basic tests, and recording data. This role often involves working independently and following written procedures, making it suitable for individuals who prefer minimal verbal interaction.

Scriptwriter (Films, Videos, Written Podcasts)

A Scriptwriter creates written content for visual or audio productions such as films, YouTube videos, or podcasts. They structure stories, write dialogue, and ensure that the script matches the tone and purpose of the content. This job requires strong writing skills and creativity.

IT Support (Chat or Ticketing Systems):

An IT Support Specialist provides technical assistance to users via written communication like chat or support tickets. They troubleshoot issues related to software, hardware, and networks, and help users solve technical problems efficiently without the need for voice communication.
