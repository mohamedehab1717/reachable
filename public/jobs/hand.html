Art Instructor (Foot Drawing or Crafting)

Teach others how to draw, paint, or craft using their feet instead of their hands. This can be done through online classes, workshops, or recorded tutorials. It’s a powerful way to inspire others facing similar challenges, and it focuses on creativity and skill rather than traditional hand use.

Radio Presenter / Podcast Host

Host shows, interviews, or podcasts. This job focuses on speaking, interacting, and engaging an audience — perfect for individuals who are articulate and love communication.

Translator

Translate written documents from one language to another. You can use assistive technology if needed. It’s a desk-based job perfect for focusing on mental skills rather than manual tasks.

Social Media Manager

Managing social media accounts for businesses or individuals. Tasks include creating posts, responding to messages, and analyzing engagement. This role can be performed using adaptive tools to assist with typing and navigation.
