Wok And Walk

Cairo, Egypt

Wok and Walk is a casual Asian fusion restaurant known for its fast service and build-your-own stir-fry experience.

Features

  • - Smooth entrance with no steps for easy wheelchair access.
  • - Spacious lifts suitable for wheelchairs and mobility aids.
  • - Ample space to move around comfortably.
  • - Restrooms adapted for disabled users.
  • - Designated parking close to the entrance.
