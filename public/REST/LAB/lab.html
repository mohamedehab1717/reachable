Espresso Lab

Cairo, Egypt

Espresso Lab is a specialty coffeehouse chain in Egypt, originating from Turkey, renowned for its high-quality coffee and inviting ambiance. With multiple branches across Cairo, including locations in New Cairo, Maadi, and Heliopolis, Espresso Lab offers a unique coffee experience for enthusiasts and casual drinkers alike.​

Features

  • -Most branches have accessible entrances and spacious seating areas.
  • -Modern rustic interiors with wooden furniture and warm lighting create a cozy atmosphere.
  • -Ideal for remote work, studying, or casual meetings with reliable internet access.
  • -Baristas are well-trained and provide personalized service.
