Al Fredo

Cairo, Egypt

Al Fredo is a cozy Italian restaurant known for its delicious pasta, wood-fired pizzas, and creamy Alfredo dishes. With a warm ambiance and friendly staff, it’s the perfect spot to enjoy authentic Italian flavors in a relaxing setting.

Features

  • - Wheelchair-accessible entrance and seating
  • - Spacious interior for easy navigation
  • - Accessible restrooms with support handles
  • - Friendly, trained staff to assist guests with special needs
  • - Soft lighting and cozy atmosphere
  • - Vegetarian and gluten-free options available
  • - Free Wi-Fi and quiet dining space

Location

Address: 6 Pyramids Road, Marriott Mena House Hotel, Giza 12556, Egypt.
