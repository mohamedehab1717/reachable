Adaptive Skiing

Available in Select Resorts Worldwide

Adaptive skiing is a winter sport designed for individuals with physical or sensory impairments. Using modified equipment such as sit-skis and outriggers, athletes of all abilities can enjoy skiing safely and competitively. It promotes independence, coordination, and adrenaline-fueled fun on the slopes.

Where to Ski

Try international destinations like Ski Dubai (UAE) or Alpine resorts in Europe offering adaptive programs and professional instructors.
