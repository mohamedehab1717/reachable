Egyptian Paralympic Clubs

Boccia is a precision ball sport similar to bocce, designed for athletes with severe physical disabilities. It is played by individuals, pairs, or teams, and involves throwing leather balls as close as possible to a white target ball (jack). It is a Paralympic sport with classifications for different types of impairments.

Where to Play

Visit local adaptive sports centers such as the Egyptian Paralympic Committee or centers affiliated with disability sports federations to find Boccia programs.
