Horus Medical and Prosthetics Company

Cairo, Egypt

​Horus Medical and Prosthetics Company, founded in 1985 by Dr. Mahrous Taha, is a prominent Egyptian manufacturer and supplier of medical and rehabilitation equipment, headquartered in Cairo. With over five decades of experience in the field, the company specializes in producing high-quality wheelchairs, artificial limbs, hospital beds, walking aids, and other healthcare products designed to support individuals with disabilities.

Products

  • -Artificial Limbs: Below-knee and above-knee prostheses, modular systems, and silicone foot prostheses.
  • -Wheelchairs: Manual, electric, and sports models.
  • -Hospital Equipment: Beds, furniture, and operating tables.
  • -Rehabilitation Products: Walking aids, commode chairs, and oxygen concentrators.
  • -Rehabilitation Equipment: Tools designed for physical therapy and patient recovery.
