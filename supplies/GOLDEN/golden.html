Golden Medic

Cairo, Egypt

Golden Medic is a medical supplies company based in New Maadi, Cairo, Egypt. Established in June 2018, the company is dedicated to providing high-quality medical equipment and supplies to residents of Maadi and surrounding areas at fair prices, with the convenience of home delivery services.

Products

  • -Wheelchairs: Manual wheelchairs suitable for daily use.
  • -Walkers and Canes: Assistive devices that provide stability and support for individuals with mobility challenges.
  • -Underpads: "Dry Plus Underpads" in sizes 180x90 cm and 90x60 cm to protect bedding and furniture.
  • -Lumbar Belts: Back support belts to assist with lower back pain or injuries.
  • -Knee and Ankle Supports: Braces and supports to aid in joint stability and mobility.
