Uber - DiDi - InDrive

Uber, DiDi, and InDrive are the leading ride-hailing apps operating in Egypt, providing easy and convenient transport services for both locals and visitors. All three platforms allow users to book rides via their mobile apps, track vehicles, and select different vehicle options based on their preferences and budget.

Features

  • - Uber offers UberAssist in some countries (though not yet in Egypt), providing special assistance to riders with disabilities. The app also allows passengers to add ride-specific instructions for drivers, which may include mobility support needs.
  • - DiDi is still in the early stages of its service in Egypt and, like Uber, does not have a dedicated disability-accessible service. However, the app allows riders to request assistance through special instructions.
  • - InDrive, a newer service, allows riders to negotiate the fare with drivers directly. While it lacks specific accessibility features, it offers flexibility in terms of cost and travel options. The app also allows users to communicate special needs to drivers, though accessibility support can depend on the driver.
