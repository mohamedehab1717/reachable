Light Rail Transit

Cairo, Egypt

LRT system in Egypt is an advanced, modern transportation solution designed to enhance urban mobility and connect various regions with a focus on accessibility

Features

  • 1. Station Accessibility
    • -Elevators (Lifts): Available in all stations for easy access between different levels.
    • -Ramps: Smooth ramps for wheelchair users to easily board and disembark.
    • -Tactile Flooring: Special flooring to help visually impaired passengers navigate the stations.
  • 2. Train Accessibility
    • -Designated Spaces for Wheelchairs: Equipped with safety belts to secure wheelchairs during the journey.
    • -Low Floors: The train floors are lowered to facilitate easier boarding and alighting for passengers with mobility challenges.
    • -Audio and Visual Announcements: These systems inform passengers about upcoming stations, ensuring better travel experience.
  • 3. Additional Services
    • -Sign Language Support: Available at Adly Mansour station, with instructional videos in sign language.
    • -Staff Training: Personnel are trained to communicate using sign language, ensuring assistance for hearing‐impaired passengers.

Location
